### Weather
- Perpetual cold.
- Gray skies, coastal fog, frozen seas.
- Stark mountains and wind-scoured plains.
- Emotionally subdued populace.

### Society
- Rationalist society.
- Suspicious of magic and superstition.
- Values science, engineering, medicine.
- Emotion seen as weakness.
- Religion marginalized.
- Reserved. Formal. Skeptical. Quietly ambitious.

### Technology Level
- Advanced surgery.
- Anatomical research.
- Experimental reanimation.
- Early industrial workshops.
- Firearms more common than most Core domains.
