### The Weaver of the Swamp Fane
- The Weaver of the Swamp Fane represents the remembrance of the past

### The Huntress of the Mountain Fane
- The Huntress of the Mountain Fane represents the acceptance of the present
- nature based death god, accept death

Her physique is well toned, like that of an Amazonian woman and her long, white hair sits upon her shoulders like snow on the cold stone of a mountain top. She has a pair of enormous, stag antlers growing from her temples, adding significantly to her height.

- shrine is the collection of monoliths behind Old Bonegrinder.

## Lycanthropy
The Ladies originally used Lycanthropy to gift their faithful followers and to curse their blasphemers. The Huntress (Mountain Fane) is responsible for wolf lycanthropes in Barovia while the Seeker (Forest Fane) is responsible for raven lycanthropes in Barovia. In their current, weakened states, they are incapable of granting or curing lycanthropy however.

## Shrines
- The people of the valley used to worship the Ladies through a series of shrines scattered across the land, some small and some large. The three most prominent shrines were located inside large stone circles that still stand during the present time of the campaign.
	- The swamp shrine is the circle of stones in Berez.
	- The mountain shrine is the circle atop Yesterhill.
	- The forest shrine is the collection of monoliths behind Old Bonegrinder.
- The worship of the Ladies usually came in the form of minor religious practices and offerings. The offerings were usually things like, "The best fruits from the first harvest" or "the antlers of a buck we killed on hunt." Sometimes the people left small, carved statues or other bits of artistic labor.
- The practices were usually communes with nature, where folk would wonder into the woods and live entirely alone in respect for the Ladies. Those who survived would return home spiritually awakened. Other rituals were long nights spent in respect and worship or village wide pilgrimages to the major shrines.

## The Fall of the Ladies
- The Ladies presided over the valley until Strahd conquered the land. Strahd, seeing the power of the fanes, desecrated the three main shrines to the ladies and reconsecrated them in his own image, giving him dominion over the valley. Now, Strahd is literally, "The Land."
- Strahd managed to do so with the knowledge from Amber Temple and the help of Vampyr
- By taking over the Fanes, Strahd has the following benefits:
	- Mountain Fane: Strahd can alter terrain in a 30ft radius around him, such as erecting walls or pillars up to 20ft in each dimension. Strahd can create an earthquake in a mile radius that destroys most homes and gives every disadvantage on saves, attacks, and checks for 1 minute for all non-flyers. (1/day).
	- Forest Fane: Strahd gains control over all wolves, bats, and beasts in Barovia, excluding Ravens. He can also tap into their senses and give commands from anywhere in Barovia.
	- Swamp Fane: Strahd regenerates 20 health per action, bonus action, or legendary action it takes, and this effect is not altered by radiant damage or running water.
	- With the combined control of all three Fanes, he controls the waters, winds, and wildlife in the valley. The only of Strahd's environmental powers that does _not_ come from the Fanes is the ever overcast sky. That comes from Vampyr.

## Reinstating the Ladies
- To reconsecrate the shrines and therefore bring back the Ladies of the Wood, certain rituals will have to be performed. You have to take their gem back to their dedicated shrine, then honor the ladies by name, then sleep with the gem on your chest. The fane would talk to the ritual performer in their sleep and requests them to do one action. If they succeed, then the fane is restored.

- To reconsecrate the shrine of the Mountain Fane at Yesterhill, someone must survive a journey through the caverns under the Gulthias Tree and prove the worth of their soul. (A dungeon of my own making detailed in a later post.)
- To reconsecrate the shrine of the Forest Fane behind Old Bonegrinder, the players must face one of their possible dark futures, to understand just how far they could have fallen.
- To reconsecrate the shrine of the Swamp Fane in Berez, someone must to kill Baba Lysaga, the witch currently poisoning the swamp fane, and present her heart at the shrine.
